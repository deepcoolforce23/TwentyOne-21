# TwentyOne-21
a recreation of the 21 vhs from resident evil 7 in python


#How to play:


Trump Cards:


Perfect draw: Takes a card from the deck that will most likely let your hand value reach the limit or get close to it
Switcharoo: Swaps a random card from your opponent's hand with a card of your choosing
Refresh: Returns all your cards to the deck and gives you 2 new cards
Betrayal: Give your opponent an extra card
17: Sets the limit to 17
24: Sets the limit to 24
27: Sets the limit to 27
Destroy: Removes the 3 cards just mentioned once used and resets limit to 21


Game loop:


You have draw to get cards, pass when you're busted or at the limit, and use trump cards to even your odds

When you win, you gain distance, while your opponent loses the same amount of distance as you gained

The game ends when somebody has negative or zero distance


Multiplayer:


You can host your own game, or join another person's game with a code, and play.
